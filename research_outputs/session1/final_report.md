# Amazon Q1 2024 Financial Summary

## Overview
Amazon.com, Inc. reported strong financial results for the first quarter of 2024, driven by double-digit growth across its primary business segments. The company experienced significant expansion in its cloud services (AWS) and its North American retail operations [1].

## Revenue Analysis
In the first quarter of 2024, Amazon's total consolidated net sales reached **$143,313 million ($143.31 billion)**. This represents a **13% year-over-year increase** compared to the $127,358 million reported in Q1 2023. When excluding the impact of foreign exchange rate changes, the growth rate remained consistent at 13% [1].

### Revenue Breakdown by Segment
The growth was distributed across all major segments, with AWS showing the strongest relative performance:

*   **North America:** Net sales increased 12% year-over-year to **$86,341 million**.
*   **International:** Net sales increased 10% year-over-year to **$31,935 million** (11% excluding foreign exchange impacts).
*   **AWS (Amazon Web Services):** Net sales grew 17% year-over-year to **$25,037 million**, reflecting continued strong demand for cloud computing and AI services [1].

## Key Takeaways
- Amazon's total revenue grew by 13% to $143.31 billion in Q1 2024.
- AWS continues to be a high-growth driver for the company, with a 17% increase in net sales.
- The company's core North American retail segment maintained double-digit growth at 12% [1].
