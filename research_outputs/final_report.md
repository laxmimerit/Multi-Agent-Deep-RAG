# Amazon Q1 2024 Financial Summary

## Overview
Amazon.com, Inc. reported strong financial results for the first quarter of 2024, ended March 31, 2024. The company demonstrated significant growth in net sales and a substantial increase in profitability compared to the same period in the previous year [1].

## Revenue Analysis
Amazon's total net sales for Q1 2024 reached **$143.3 billion**, representing a **13% increase** compared with $127.4 billion in the first quarter of 2023 [1]. 

The growth was distributed across its primary segments:
*   **North America Segment:** Sales increased 12% year-over-year to $86.3 billion.
*   **International Segment:** Sales increased 10% year-over-year to $31.9 billion (or 11% excluding foreign exchange impacts).
*   **AWS Segment:** Sales increased 17% year-over-year to $25.0 billion, reflecting continued momentum in cloud services and AI demand [1].

## Profitability Metrics
The company saw a dramatic improvement in its bottom-line performance during the quarter:
*   **Operating Income:** Increased to **$15.3 billion** in Q1 2024, compared with $4.8 billion in Q1 2023 [1].
*   **Net Income:** Rose to **$10.4 billion** ($0.98 per diluted share), a significant jump from $3.2 billion ($0.31 per diluted share) in the prior-year period [1].

## Key Takeaways
*   **Double-Digit Growth:** Amazon maintained a 13% revenue growth rate, driven by strengths in North America and a re-acceleration in AWS growth.
*   **Operational Efficiency:** Operating income more than tripled year-over-year, indicating successful cost management and improved fulfillment efficiency.
*   **AWS Strength:** The 17% growth in AWS marks a strong performance as enterprises integrate generative AI capabilities.
