### Sources for Theme 2: Apple Margin Trends (2023-2024)

#### SEC Filings (Historical Data)
1.  **Apple Inc. Form 10-K (Fiscal Year Ended September 30, 2023)**
    *   **Source File:** apple 10-k 2023.md

2.  **Apple Inc. Form 10-Q (Third Quarter Ended June 29, 2024)**
    *   **Source File:** apple 10-q q2 2024.md (Note: Metadata labeled as Q2 but content includes 9-month data ending June 29, 2024, which corresponds to the third fiscal quarter).
    *   **Page 4:** Condensed Consolidated Statements of Operations (Unaudited) for the three and nine months ended June 29, 2024.
    *   **Page 19:** Specific tables for Products and Services gross margin percentages for quarterly and nine-month comparisons (2024 vs. 2023).

#### Market Data (Current Information)
3.  **Yahoo Finance (Live Data)**
    *   **Data Point:** Analyst recommendations for Apple (AAPL) showing 29 "Buy/Strong Buy", 15 "Hold", and 4 "Sell/Strong Sell" ratings.

#### Key Metrics & Figures
- **FY 2023 Total Gross Margin:** 44.1%
- **FY 2023 Operating Margin:** 29.8%
- **9M 2024 Total Gross Margin:** 46.2%
- **9M 2024 Operating Margin:** 31.6%
- **Services GM (Q3 2024):** 74.0%
- **Products GM (Q3 2024):** 35.3%
- **Currency Impact:** Weakness in foreign currencies cited as a primary headwind to sales and products gross margin in both 2023 and 2024.
- **OpEx Drivers:** Increases in Research and Development (R&D) driven by headcount-related expenses.
