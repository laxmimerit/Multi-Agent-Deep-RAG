SOURCES FOR THEME 3: Performance Breakdown by Key Product Segments (2023-2024)

1. SEC Filing: Apple Inc. Form 10-K for the fiscal year ended September 30, 2023
   - Page 25: Table "Net sales by category" for years 2021, 2022, 2023.
   - Page 25: Commentary on iPhone, Mac, iPad, Wearables, and Services performance drivers for 2023.
   - Data Points: 2023 iPhone revenue $200.6B, Mac $29.4B, iPad $28.3B, Wearables $39.8B, Services $85.2B.

2. SEC Filing: Apple Inc. Form 10-K for the fiscal year ended September 28, 2024
   - Page 26: Table "Net sales by category" for years 2022, 2023, 2024.
   - Page 26: Narrative explanation of segment changes in 2024.
   - Data Points: 2024 iPhone revenue $201.2B, Mac $30.0B, iPad $26.7B, Wearables $37.0B, Services $96.2B.

3. SEC Filing: Apple Inc. Form 10-Q for the quarterly period ended June 29, 2024 (Q3 2024)
   - Page 18: Quarterly net sales by category comparing 2024 vs 2023.
   - Page 18: Discussion of 24% growth in iPad revenue during Q3 2024.
   - Data Points: Q3 2024 iPad revenue $7.16B.

4. SEC Filing: Apple Inc. Form 10-Q for the quarterly period ended March 30, 2024 (Q2 2024)
   - Page 18: Three and six-month period breakdowns for 2024.
   - Data Points: Q2 2024 iPhone revenue $45.96B, Services $23.87B.

KEY QUOTES:
- "Services net sales increased during 2024 compared to 2023 due primarily to higher net sales from advertising, the App Store and cloud services." (10-K 2024)
- "iPhone net sales decreased 2%... during 2023 compared to 2022 due to lower net sales of non-Pro iPhone models." (10-K 2023)
- "iPad net sales increased during the third quarter of 2024... due primarily to higher net sales of iPad Pro and iPad Air." (10-Q Q3 2024)
