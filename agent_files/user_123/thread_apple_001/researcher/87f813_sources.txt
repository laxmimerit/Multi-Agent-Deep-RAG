### Sources for Theme 4: Regional Performance (2023-2024)

1. **Apple Inc. Form 10-K for the Fiscal Year Ended September 28, 2024**
   - Source: SEC Filing
   - Key Data: Full year 2024 regional revenue table, 2023/2022 comparisons, and management discussion of regional drivers (Services growth in Americas/Europe, iPhone decline in China).
   - Snippet: "Americas net sales increased during 2024 compared to 2023 due primarily to higher net sales of Services... Greater China net sales decreased during 2024 compared to 2023 due primarily to lower net sales of iPhone and iPad."

2. **Apple Inc. Form 10-Q for the Quarterly Period Ended June 29, 2024 (Q3 2024)**
   - Source: SEC Filing
   - Key Data: Quarterly regional performance for the three and nine months ended June 2024.
   - Snippet: "Greater China net sales decreased during the third quarter of 2024 compared to the third quarter of 2023 due primarily to lower net sales of iPhone."

3. **Apple Inc. Form 10-Q for the Quarterly Period Ended March 30, 2024 (Q2 2024)**
   - Source: SEC Filing
   - Key Data: Breakdown of net sales for the second quarter and first six months of 2024.
   - Snippet: "Americas net sales were relatively flat during the second quarter of 2024... with higher net sales of Services offset by lower net sales of iPhone and iPad."

4. **Regional Definitions (from 10-K)**
   - Americas: North and South America.
   - Europe: European countries, India, Middle East, and Africa.
   - Greater China: China mainland, Hong Kong, and Taiwan.
   - Rest of Asia Pacific: Australia and Asian countries not included in other segments.
