### Sources for Theme 4: Apple Geographic Performance 2023-2024

**Source 1: Apple Inc. 2024 Form 10-K (Annual Report)**
- **Scope:** Full fiscal year 2024 (ended Sept 28, 2024) and 2023 comparison.
- **Key Data:** 
  - Segment revenue table (Americas, Europe, Greater China, Japan, Rest of Asia Pacific).
  - Geographic definitions: Europe includes India, Middle East, and Africa.
  - Qualitative analysis of China's decline (iPhone/iPad weakness, currency).
- **URL:** SEC EDGAR / Apple Investor Relations

**Source 2: Apple Inc. Q2 2024 Form 10-Q (Quarterly Report)**
- **Scope:** Nine-month period ended June 29, 2024.
- **Key Data:** 
  - Breakdown of revenue growth by region for the first nine months.
  - Mention of foreign currency impact (RMB, Yen) as a headwind.
- **URL:** SEC EDGAR / Apple Investor Relations

**Source 3: Apple Inc. Q1 2024 Form 10-Q (Quarterly Report)**
- **Scope:** Three-month period ended Dec 30, 2023.
- **Key Data:**
  - Initial 2024 fiscal year performance and comparison to the 53-week fiscal 2023.
- **URL:** SEC EDGAR / Apple Investor Relations

**Key Snippets:**
- "Greater China net sales decreased during 2024 compared to 2023 due primarily to lower net sales of iPhone and iPad. The weakness in the renminbi relative to the U.S. dollar had an unfavorable year-over-year impact..." (10-K 2024)
- "Europe net sales increased during 2024 compared to 2023 due primarily to higher net sales of Services and iPhone." (10-K 2024)
- "The Company manages its business primarily on a geographic basis... Europe includes European countries, as well as India, the Middle East and Africa." (10-K 2024)
