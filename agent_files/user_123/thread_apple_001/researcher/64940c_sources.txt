### Sources: Apple Product Segment Performance (2023-2024)

**Source 1: Apple Inc. FY 2024 Form 10-K (Annual Report)**
- URL: SEC EDGAR Database (Apple 10-K 2024)
- Key Data:
    - iPhone Revenue (2024): $201,183M
    - Services Revenue (2024): $96,169M
    - Mac Revenue (2024): $29,984M
    - iPad Revenue (2024): $26,694M
    - Wearables, Home and Accessories (2024): $37,005M
- Notes: Confirms the 2% increase in total net sales for FY 2024. Highlights advertising, App Store, and cloud services as the drivers for the 13% Services growth.

**Source 2: Apple Inc. FY 2023 Form 10-K (Annual Report)**
- URL: SEC EDGAR Database (Apple 10-K 2023)
- Key Data:
    - iPhone Revenue (2023): $200,583M
    - Services Revenue (2023): $85,200M
    - Mac Revenue (2023): $29,357M
    - iPad Revenue (2023): $28,300M
    - Wearables... (2023): $39,845M
- Notes: Documents the 27% decline in Mac revenue and 2% decline in iPhone revenue for the 2023 fiscal year.

**Source 3: Apple Q3 2024 Form 10-Q (Quarterly Report)**
- Period: Three and Nine Months Ended June 29, 2024
- Key Data:
    - iPad revenue grew 24% in Q3 2024 compared to Q3 2023 ($7,162M vs $5,791M).
    - Services revenue grew 14% in the same quarter ($24,213M vs $21,213M).

**Source 4: Apple Q2 2024 Form 10-Q (Quarterly Report)**
- Period: Three and Six Months Ended March 30, 2024
- Key Data:
    - iPhone revenue fell 10% in the specific quarter (Q2 2024) from $51.3B to $46.0B.
    - Mac revenue showed a 4% increase in the same quarter.

**Source 5: Apple Q1 2024 (8-K Press Release)**
- Date: February 1, 2024
- Key Quote: "Today Apple is reporting revenue growth for the December quarter fueled by iPhone sales, and an all-time revenue record in Services," said Tim Cook. Active device base surpassed 2.2 billion.
