### Sources for Theme 1: Revenue and Net Income

1. **Apple Inc. Form 10-K (Fiscal Year 2023)**
   - **Source File:** apple 10-k 2023.md
   - **Key Data:** 
     - Page 23: Total net sales ($383.3B) and net income ($97.0B) for 2023.
     - Page 31: Consolidated Statements of Operations showing 2023 vs 2022 vs 2021.
     - Page 38: Net sales disaggregated by products and services.

2. **Apple Inc. Form 10-Q (Third Quarter Fiscal 2024 - "Q2" report in internal naming)**
   - **Source File:** apple 10-q q2 2024.md (Note: This file contains data for the nine months ended June 29, 2024)
   - **Key Data:**
     - Page 4: Nine months ended June 29, 2024 Net Sales ($296,105M) and Net Income ($79,000M).

3. **Yahoo Finance (Live Market Data)**
   - **Query:** Apple annual revenue and net income for fiscal year 2023 and 2024 quarterly results
   - **Key Data:** Q4 FY2024 Revenue ($94.93B) and Net Income ($14.736B).
   - **Context:** The net income for Q4 2024 reflects the impact of the European General Court decision on state aid.

4. **Calculation Notes:**
   - FY 2024 Total Sales = $296,105M (9 months) + $94,930M (Q4) = $391,035M.
   - FY 2024 Net Income = $79,000M (9 months) + $14,736M (Q4) = $93,736M.
